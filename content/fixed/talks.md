## [How To Run on Three Clouds at Once, and When Not To](https://qconlondon.com/presentation/mar2026/how-run-three-clouds-once-and-when-not) {#three-clouds}

QCon London — March 2026 (with Kevin Holditch)

![How to Run on Three Clouds at Once, and When Not To — title slide](/img/qcon-2026-title.png)

Form3 handles a significant portion of UK bank transfers, so outages are highly visible. This talk covers our journey to active/active/active multi-cloud infrastructure across AWS, GCP, and Azure — design trade-offs, operational costs, dead-ends, and hard lessons from several years of running critical financial infrastructure. We also cover our US market expansion, where the multi-cloud pitch didn't land as expected.
